Every Bluetooth device within ~10 meters is continuously broadcasting its presence — phones, headphones, watches, trackers, medical devices. Each one sends packets into the air up to 10 times per second, whether connected to anything or not.
this is how retail stores track your path through the building. Fixed BLE scanners log your MAC address as you pass each aisle. Dwell time at each product is recorded. No app required on your end. The Jida scooter app had its DEVICE_KEY hardcoded globally — anyone with the APK can unlock any Jida scooter worldwide.

▶ how does this work?

BLE devices advertise on channels 37, 38, 39 (2.402 / 2.426 / 2.480 GHz) up to 10× per second. Each packet contains MAC address, device name, signal strength (RSSI), and optional manufacturer data.

RSSI is in dBm. -40 dBm = very close. -90 dBm = edge of range. Every 6 dBm ≈ 2× distance change. Web Bluetooth API lets a browser listen with user permission — no pairing needed.
